Scene 1: Yongquan and a New Friend

Yongquan arrives at a new school and meets a shy classmate. He greets him with a smile and kindness, encouraging him to play together.

Scene 2: Yongquan's Help

On the playground, Yongquan notices a little girl has fallen. He immediately rushes over to help her, gently lifting her up and reassuring her not to be afraid.

Scene 3: Yongquan Learns to Share

Yongquan brings his favorite toy to school but notices a classmate doesn’t have one. He decides to share his toy.

Scene 4: Yongquan and Teamwork

Yongquan and his classmates participate in a team competition. He learns to listen, cooperate, and together they win.

Scene 5: Yongquan’s Responsibility

Yongquan takes care of the class plant, watering it every day. He learns the importance of responsibility as the plant grows.

Scene 6: Yongquan Faces Challenges with Courage

Yongquan participates in a drawing competition. At first, his drawing isn't great, but he doesn’t give up and finishes a picture he is proud of.

Scene 7: Yongquan Learns Patience

Yongquan and his friends play a difficult puzzle game. He learns to stay calm and patiently solve the puzzle.

Scene 8: Yongquan’s Creative Problem Solving

Yongquan and his classmates face a challenge to create an eco-friendly art project. He comes up with a great idea using recycled materials.

Scene 9: Yongquan’s Courage

Yongquan takes the lead role in the school play. Despite his nervousness, he gathers courage and performs wonderfully.

Scene 10: Yongquan’s Gratitude

At the end of the story, Yongquan thanks everyone who helped him and gives them a thank-you card he drew himself.
